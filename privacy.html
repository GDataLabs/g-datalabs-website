Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data and respect your rights. Ask our AI assistant to explain any section in simple terms.

1. Information We Collect

G-Data Labs collects information to provide and improve our services while maintaining the highest standards of privacy protection.

Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and account preferences
  • Contact Information: Communication data when you reach out to us
  • Profile Information: Information you choose to provide in your profile

Biometric and Research Data

  • EEG Data: Brain activity patterns (only with explicit consent for research)
  • Facial Expression Data: Emotion recognition data for AI research
  • Eye Tracking Data: Gaze patterns and attention metrics
  • Galvanic Skin Response: Stress and arousal indicators

Usage Data

  • Platform interactions and feature usage
  • Device information and browser data
  • Session logs and performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for specific, transparent purposes that align with our mission of ethical AI development.

Service Provision

  • Account management and authentication
  • Personalized AI literacy education
  • Workshop and consultation scheduling
  • Platform functionality and features

Research and Development

  • Advancing human-AI interaction research
  • Developing ethical AI frameworks
  • Improving biometric data analysis techniques
  • Publishing anonymized research findings

Communication

  • Service updates and announcements
  • Educational content and workshop notifications
  • Support and customer service

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

G-Data Labs maintains strict control over data sharing and only shares information under specific, transparent circumstances.

We DO NOT Share:

  • Personal information with third-party advertisers
  • Biometric data with unauthorized parties
  • Individual user data for commercial purposes
  • Any data without proper consent and legal basis

We MAY Share:

  • Anonymized Research Data: For academic publication and scientific advancement
  • Aggregated Statistics: Non-identifying data trends and insights
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or legal process
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners under strict data processing agreements

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Advanced encryption for data storage (AES-256)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication requirements

Organizational Safeguards

  • Limited access on a need-to-know basis
  • Employee training on data protection protocols
  • Regular security awareness programs
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have significant control over your personal information and how it's used.

Data Rights

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

Consent Management

  • Withdraw consent for biometric data collection at any time
  • Opt out of research participation
  • Control marketing communications
  • Manage data sharing preferences

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Until account deletion plus 30 days
  • Biometric Data: 5 years for research purposes (with consent)
  • Usage Data: 2 years for service improvement
  • Communication Data: 3 years for support purposes

Secure Deletion

When data is deleted, we use secure deletion methods to ensure it cannot be recovered or reconstructed.

7. International Data Transfers

G-Data Labs operates globally and may transfer data across borders while maintaining privacy protections.

Transfer Safeguards

  • Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Additional safeguards for sensitive data
  • Regular monitoring of transfer mechanisms

8. Children's Privacy

G-Data Labs does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Age Verification

  • Age verification during account creation
  • Parental consent for users under 18 in research studies
  • Special protections for educational programs

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@g-datalabs.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@g-datalabs.com
  • Address: G-Data Labs Privacy Department
  • Response Time: Within 30 days of verified requests
