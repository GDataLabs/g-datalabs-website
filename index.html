Our Mission

To shepherd the interaction of Humans and AI for the safety and benefit of all people by researching and developing responsible AI frameworks, providing accessible AI literacy, and implementing effective AI integrations to organizations across a wide range of industries.

Our Vision

We believe every person generates value and deserves to understand and benefit from AI technology.

Our Values

Ethical AI development with explicit consent, transparency, and fair compensation for data contributors.

Our Goal

Foster new careers, entrepreneurship, and ownership in the AI economy through education and empowerment.
